1. Who We Are

Welcome to Hebron. We are a team of technical writers and technology enthusiasts dedicated to demystifying modern hardware. Our goal is to create clear, easy-to-understand educational content that helps users understand how their devices work.

Our Mission: To provide accessible, jargon-free explanations of printer technology, setup concepts, and general maintenance logic.

2. What We Do

We research common technical issues and summarize the standard industry solutions into educational guides. We explain the "Why" and "How" behind printer setup, wireless networking, and driver installation theories.

3. What We Do NOT Do

To avoid confusion, it is important to clarify our role:

  • We are not a customer support agency.
  • We do not represent any hardware brand (like HP, Canon, etc.).
  • We do not fix printers remotely or take control of your computer.
  • We do not sell software or drivers.

4. Transparency

We believe in full transparency. This website is an independent project and is supported by our own resources. We maintain strict independence from manufacturers to provide unbiased educational information.
